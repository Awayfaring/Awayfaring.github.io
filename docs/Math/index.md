# Math

This is a page for mathematical content.

Here's an example of inline math: $10x$

And here's an example of display math:

$$
\frac{d}{dx}e^x = e^x
$$

You can also use other delimiters:
- Inline: \( f(x) = x^2 \)
- Display: \[ \int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx \]
